📖 Islam Mein Daan Ki Qismein

Quran ke hawalo (references) ke sath poori detail.

1. ZAKAT (Sabse Zaroori Daan - Farz)

Zakat har us musalman par farz hai jiske paas ek tay shuda miqdar (Nisab) ke barabar maal ek saal tak jama ho.

📖 Quran ka Hawala: Para: 1 (Alif-Laam-Meem) | Surah Al-Baqarah (2), Aayat No: 43

وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ

Tarjuma: "Aur namaz qaim karo aur Zakat ada karo."

📝 Aasan Calculation: Hanafi nisab 612.36 gram chandi hai. Misaal ke taur par, ghar mein biwi ke paas 650 gram chandi hai, toh yeh nisab se zyada hai. Isliye aapko saal mein ek baar is chandi ki aaj ki total value ka 2.5% nikal kar gareebon mein bantna hoga. Yahi rule cash aur sone par bhi lagta hai.

💡 Zakat Kise Dein? Para: 10 (Wa'lamu) | Surah At-Tawbah (9), Aayat No: 60

Tarjuma: "Beshak Zakat toh faqreeron (gareebon), miskeenon (mohtajon), zakat ikhatta karne walo, aur jinka dil Islam ki taraf mائل karna ho, aur gardanein chhudane (ghulamon ko azaad karne) mein, aur karzdaron ke liye, aur Allah ki raah mein, aur musafiron ke liye hai."

2. USHR (Fasal ya Kheti ka Daan - Farz)

Agar aap kisan hain aur kheti karte hain, toh fasal katne par Zakat ki tarah 'Ushr' nikalna farz hai.

📖 Quran ka Hawala: Para: 8 (Wa Lau Annana) | Surah Al-An'am (6), Aayat No: 141

وَآتُوا حَقَّهُ يَوْمَ حَصَادِهِ

Tarjuma: "Aur jis din fasal kate, uska haq (Zakat/Ushr) ada karo."

📝 Aasan Calculation: Agar khet mein baarish ke paani se fasal hui hai, toh paidaish ka 10% daan dena hai. Agar aapne apne paiso se motor/tubewell chala kar paani diya hai, toh paidaish ka 5% daan dena hai.

3. FIDYAH (Roza na rakh paane ki Majboori ka Daan)

Agar koi shakhs budhape ya aisi beemari ki wajah se Ramzan ke roze nahi rakh sakta jiske theek hone ki umeed nahi hai, toh us par har roze ke badle daan (Fidyah) dena lazmi hai.

📖 Quran ka Hawala: Para: 2 (Sayaqool) | Surah Al-Baqarah (2), Aayat No: 184

وَعَلَى الَّذِينَ يُطِيقُونَهُ فِدْيَةٌ طَعَامُ مِسْكِينٍ

Tarjuma: "Aur jo log iski taqat na rakhein (yaani bohut mushkil se rakh sakein/beemari ki wajah se), unpar fidyah hai ek miskeen (gareeb) ko khana khilana."

📝 Aasan Calculation: Ek roza chhootne par ek gareeb ko do waqt ka pet bhar khana khilana, ya ek 'Fitrane' ke barabar rakam (kareeb 2 kg gehun ka daam) dena.

4. KAFFARAH (Galti ya Jurmane ka Daan)

Agar koi jaan-boojh kar Allah ki kasam khaye aur use tod de, toh use is gunah ki maafi ke liye daan (Kaffarah) dena hota hai.

📖 Quran ka Hawala: Para: 7 (Wa Iza Sami'u) | Surah Al-Ma'idah (5), Aayat No: 89

فَكَفَّارَتُهُ إِطْعَامُ عَشَرَةِ مَسَاكِينَ مِنْ أَوْسَطِ مَا تُطْعِمُونَ أَهْلِيكُمْ أَوْ كِسْوَتُهُمْ

Tarjuma: "Toh iska kaffarah (kasam todne ka) 10 miskeenon ko ausat darje ka (average) khana khilana hai jo tum apne ghar walon ko khilate ho, ya unhe kapde pehnana hai."

📝 Aasan Calculation: 10 gareebon ko do waqt ka khana khilana ya 10 gareebon ko jode kapde dena.

5. AAM SADAQAH (Apni Khushi aur Marzi ka Daan)

Yeh wo daan hai jo aap kabhi bhi, kitna bhi apni khushi se de sakte hain. Allah ise bohut pasand karta hai.

📖 Quran ka Hawala: Para: 3 (Tilkal Rusulu) | Surah Al-Baqarah (2), Aayat No: 261

مَّثَلُ الَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ كَمَثَلِ حَبَّةٍ أَنبَتَتْ سَبْعَ سَنَابِلَ فِي كُلِّ سُنبُلَةٍ مِّائَةُ حَبَّةٍ

Tarjuma: "Jo log apne maal Allah ki raah mein kharch karte hain, unki misaal us daane jaisi hai jisne 7 baaliyan ugaayi aur har baali mein 100 daane hon. Aur Allah jiske liye chahta hai (sawab ko) aur badha deta hai."

📝 Aasan Calculation: Iski koi limit nahi hai. Yeh raaste mein zarooratmand ko paise dena, pyase ko paani pilana, ya Masjid/Madrase mein madad karna sab isme aata hai.
⚠️ Ek Zaroori Note: Eid par diya jane wala Sadaqah al-Fitr (Fitrana) aur Bakreed par ki jane wali Qurbani dono 'Wajib' hain. Inka hukm seedhe taur par Quran ki aayaton mein is naam se nahi hai, balki Nabi (SAW) ki Sahih Hadeeson aur unki sunnat se sabit hai, jise manna har musalman par lazmi hai.
