📖 Islam Mein Daan Ki Qismein

Islam mein (Hanafi Fiqh ke mutabiq) daan ya charity ka ek poora system hai jise alag-alag hisson mein banta gaya hai.

1. FARZ (Obligatory Charity)

Yeh wo daan hai jo ek khush-haal musalman par har haal mein farz hai, iske na dene par gunah milta hai.

  • Zakat (Maal ki Zakat): Saal bhar ki bachat aur assets par 2.5% daan karna. Misaal ke taur par, agar kisi ke paas nisab se zyada chandi/sona hai, toh total value par 2.5% zakat dena farz hoga.
  • Ushr (Faslon ki Zakat): Kheti se hone wali paidaish par (Baarish ke paani par 10%, apne kharch ke paani par 5%).
📖 Quran: "Namaz qaim karo aur Zakat ada karo." (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:43) 📖 Quran: "Aur jis din fasal kate, uska haq (Zakat/Ushr) ada karo." (Surah Al-An'am 6:141)

2. WAJIB (Necessary Charity)

Yeh Farz ke qareeb hota hai, iska ada karna bhi zaroori hai.

  • Sadaqah al-Fitr (Fitrana): Ramzan ki galtiyon ko paak karne aur gareebon ki Eid ki khushi ke liye. (Kareeb 1.75 se 2 kg gehun ke barabar).
  • Qurbani (Udhiyah): Eid-ul-Adha par. Har us shakhs par wajib hai jiske paas Zakat ke nisab ke barabar daulat ho.
🕌 Hadees: Rasoolullah (SAW) ne Sadqatul-Fitr wajib qarar diya hai... (Sahih al-Bukhari 1503) 🕌 Hadees: "Jiske paas gunjaish ho aur wo qurbani na kare, wo hamari eidgah ke qareeb na aaye." (Sunan Ibn Majah 3123)

3. KAFFARAH aur FIDYAH

Kisi ibadat ke chhootne ya galti ke kaffare (penalty) ke taur par diya jata hai.

  • Fidyah: Beemari ya budhape ki wajah se roza na rakh paane par (Ek roze ka fidyah = 1 Fitrana).
  • Kaffarah: Kasam todne ka kaffarah 10 gareebon ko khana khilana. Jaan-boojh kar roza todne ka kaffarah 60 gareebon ko khana khilana hai.
📖 Quran: "Aur jo log iski taqat na rakhein, wo fidyah mein ek miskeen ko khana khilayein." (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:184) 📖 Quran: (Kasam ka Kaffarah) 10 gareebon ko do waqt ka khana khilana ya unhe kapde pehnana hai. (Surah Al-Ma'idah 5:89)

4. NAFL / MUSTAHAB

Apni khushi aur haisiyat ke hisaab se kabhi bhi kiya jane wala daan.

  • Sadaqah Jariyah: Waqf karna (Masjid, kuan, ped lagana) jiska sawab marne ke baad bhi milta rahe.
  • Aqeeqah: Bacha paida hone ki khushi mein shukrane ke taur par janwar zibah karna.
  • Aam Sadaqah (Lillah): Kisi bhi zarooratmand ki madad karna, ya sirf muskurana bhi sadaqah hai.
🕌 Hadees: "Jab insaan mar jata hai toh uske aamal ka silsila khatam ho jata hai sivaye teen cheezon ke: Sadaqah Jariyah, Ilm, ya Nek aulad..." (Sahih Muslim 1631) 🕌 Hadees: "Tumhara apne bhai ke samne muskurana bhi sadaqah hai." (Jami at-Tirmidhi 1956)
